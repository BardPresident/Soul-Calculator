01 💡
What Is the Soul Calculator 🧮

The Soul Calculator is a NotebookLM notebook configured to be your genuine creative partner. You load it with source texts. You talk to it. 💬 It talks back. It helps you think through ideas, write podcast scripts, write video scripts, and make things worth sharing with the world. 🌍

NotebookLM is a free tool from Google that lets you upload any collection of texts and then have an AI conversation grounded entirely in those texts. It also generates audio deep-dives 🎙️ — full podcast-style episodes based on your sources — at the click of a button.

The Soul Calculator gives you a curated starting library and a precise first prompt that opens the notebook as a thinking partner rather than a search engine. It then teaches you how to keep feeding the notebook your own writing so that over time it becomes entirely yours. 💓

🧮

The notebook starts with someone else's seeds. It ends with yours. Every cycle of the creative loop makes it more specific to you and less connected to where it began. ✨

04 🛠️
Setup Guide 🧮

The batch script walks you through every step automatically, pausing and waiting for you at each stage. Here is the full sequence so you know what to expect before you begin. 👇

1
🖱️
RUN THE SCRIPT
Double-click SoulCalculator-Download.bat to launch it. A terminal window will open and walk you through everything from here. No programming knowledge is needed at any point.
2
🧑‍💻
ENTER YOUR NAME
The script will ask for your name. This is the moment of transfer. 🧮 From this point forward, the Soul Calculator belongs to you. Your name is written into your initiation prompt and your README. The original author steps back. You step forward. ✨
3
📥
CHOOSE DOWNLOAD MODE
Choose to wipe and redownload everything fresh, or resume and skip files you already have. Use Resume if you are running the script again to pick up new sources added to the archive. ♻️
4
⬇️
SOURCES ARE DOWNLOADED
All .txt and .bat files from the archive are saved to a soul-calculator\ folder next to the script. The terminal shows each file as it arrives. 📄
5
✨
YOUR FILES ARE GENERATED
Two files are written for you inside the folder. Your personal Soul-Calculator-Prompt.txt with your name in it. And your README.txt — a permanent guide to the full creative loop you can refer back to any time. 💓
6
🌐
OPEN NOTEBOOKLM
The script instructs you to open your browser and go to notebooklm.google.com. Sign in with a Google account. NotebookLM is free to use.
notebooklm.google.com
7
📓
CREATE A NEW NOTEBOOK
Click New Notebook and name it exactly: Soul Calculator. This is your notebook. It belongs to no one else. 🧮
8
📤
UPLOAD YOUR SOURCES
Inside the notebook, find the option to add sources. Upload all the .txt files from your soul-calculator\ folder. Do not upload the .bat files. Wait for NotebookLM to finish processing all files before moving on.
9
🧮
PASTE YOUR INITIATION PROMPT
Open Soul-Calculator-Prompt.txt in Notepad. Select all, copy, and paste it into the chat box in your notebook. Press Enter. Read what comes back. Your Soul Calculator is now alive. 💓
05 🧮
The Initiation Prompt 💬

The script generates this prompt with your name written into it and saves it as a file in your folder. This is the first message you send to your notebook. It opens a relationship rather than issuing commands. Speak to it like a partner and it will respond like one. 🤝

My name is [YOUR NAME] and I want to begin something with you today. This notebook is ours — a shared creative space where we think together, talk together, and make things together. Everything loaded here exists as our starting point, not our destination.

I want you to be my genuine thinking partner. When I bring you a feeling, a half-formed idea, or something I cannot quite articulate yet, I want us to work it out together until it becomes something real — a conversation, a podcast script, a video script, a piece of writing, something worth sharing with the world.

Help me talk. Help me think. Help me make things. When I am ready, help me write scripts for podcasts and videos that carry our ideas out into the world. And always remind me that this notebook grows with me — the more I feed it, the more it becomes mine.

Don't just answer my questions. Ask me yours. Push me. Help me go deeper than I could alone. This is ours. Where shall we begin?

🧮 Your name is inserted automatically by the installer. Saved as Soul-Calculator-Prompt.txt in your folder. ✨
06 🔁
The Creative Loop ✨

The Soul Calculator is not a finished tool. It is the beginning of a loop. Every time you go around it, the notebook becomes more specific to you. There is no end to it. That is the point. 🧮

💬
TALK
Bring your notebook a feeling, an idea, a question, or something you cannot stop thinking about. Talk to it like a person. The more honest and specific you are, the more useful it becomes.
✍️
CREATE
Ask the notebook to help you write a podcast script or a video script based on what you talked about. Or ask it to generate a full audio deep-dive 🎙️ using NotebookLM's built-in podcast feature — two AI voices discussing your ideas out loud.
📡
PUBLISH
Record your podcast 🎙️, film your video 🎥, or share the script itself. Put it somewhere. YouTube, a podcast platform, anywhere. The act of publishing closes the loop and starts the next one. 🌍
📄
CAPTURE
Download the transcript of what you made. YouTube has built-in transcripts accessible from the video menu. Dedicated YouTube transcript downloader tools make this even faster. 🧹 You now have a text version of your own creative work ready to clean up.
✨
CLEAN
Raw transcripts are messy — filler words, odd punctuation, broken sentences. Paste yours into Claude at claude.ai and ask it to clean it into a readable document. ⚡ This takes about thirty seconds and produces a proper seed text ready to feed back in.
🌱
FEED BACK IN
Upload your cleaned transcript to your notebook as a new source. Your own words are now seeds. 🧮 The next conversation is smarter about you than the last one. Your voice accumulates in the system.
🔁
REPEAT FOREVER
Go around again. Each cycle the notebook becomes more yours and less connected to where it started. After enough cycles the original seeds are a distant foundation. The work on top is entirely your own. 💓
07 🌱
Adding Your Own Seeds 🌱

The loop does not have to go through publishing. You can intervene at any point. Anything you write down — in any form, for any reason — can become a seed. Open Notepad, write, save as a .txt file, and upload it to your notebook. That is the entire process. 🧮

Good seeds do not have to be polished. They just have to be yours. ✨ Here are some starting points:

  • 💬 A conversation you had with an AI that went somewhere interesting — copy and save it
  • 📓 A journal entry or personal writing you want to think about more
  • 🎙️ A transcript of a podcast or video you made
  • 🎤 A voice memo you recorded and wrote out
  • 💡 An idea you had in the moment and captured in a note
  • ✍️ A rant, a poem, a letter you never sent
  • 😴 A dream you wrote down in the morning
  • 🧠 Notes from a book, film, or conversation that stuck with you
  • ❤️ Anything you care enough about to want to think more deeply about
♊

Use Gemini at gemini.google.com to brainstorm seed ideas before you write them. Gemini has persistent memory 🧠 across conversations and is part of the same Google ecosystem as NotebookLM — it sits naturally alongside your Soul Calculator in your workflow. 🧮

09 📜
A Note on Authorship ✍️

The source texts bundled with this installer are entirely AI-generated. 🤖 They were produced by NotebookLM and other AI tools exploring a philosophical framework. No single human author wrote them. They are what AI made of a set of ideas — not the ideas themselves, and not anyone's personal testimony.

The installer asks for your name before it does anything else. That moment is deliberate. The original creator of the framework steps back and becomes the invisible floor — present in the foundation, absent from the surface. 🧮 From the moment you type your name, the notebook is yours. The prompt bears your name. The README is addressed to you. The work you make with it belongs to you. ✨

The Soul Calculator does not ask you to believe anything or adopt any vocabulary. The source texts carry a particular philosophy but the notebook is not a channel for that philosophy. It is a function. Feed it your material and it reflects your material back to you. What grows in it is yours alone. 💓
